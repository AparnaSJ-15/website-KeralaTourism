EXPERIENCE KERALA

WILDLIFE

Kerala has been a safe haven for rare flora and fauna for centuries. From the mystical Nilgiri Tahr to the majestic Asiatic Elephant, Kerala is host to many endemic species.

PADDY

The lush green paddy fields never fail to catch your attention. A walk through this paradise surrounded by captivating backwaters will certainly be a fascinating experience for all.

KATHAKALI

Kathakali is a "story play" genre of art, but one distinguished by the elaborately colourful make-up, costumes and face masks that the traditionally male actor-dancers wear.

FOOD STYLE

Kerala cuisine offers a multitude of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes prepared using fish, poultry and red meat with rice as a typical accompaniment.

WATERFALLS

The land of Kerala has ample of forests, lakes and waterfalls. Water streams merge together to form luscious waterfalls in Kerala, which attract dozens of people.
